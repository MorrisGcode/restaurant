OUR STORY

Munchies Chicken & Chips Restaurantèis a fast food restaurant located in Tabere Cres, Nairobi, Kenya. They are open from 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM on weekdays and from 8:00 AM to 9:00 PM on weekends. They offer takeout, delivery, dine-in, curbside pickup, and lunch. They also serve coffee and are good for children.
Mission: To provide delicious, affordable, and convenient fast food to our customers.
Vision: To be the leading fast food restaurant in Kenya, known for our exceptional quality and service.
